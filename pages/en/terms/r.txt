==== root ====
  * //root// has always at least two significations in linux
  * //root// is the pseudonym of the administrator of the all system (see more under  [[en:terms:S?&#su|su]], [[en:terms:S?&#sudo|sudo]] and [[en:terms:C?&#chroot|chroot]])
  * //root// is the name of the home directory of the administrator and is the place for important ressources for the all system; for this reason the directory /home has to be always in the same [[en:terms:P?&#partition|partition]] as the [[en:terms:M?&#main_directory|main directory /]]
